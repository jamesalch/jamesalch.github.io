Apple AirPods

Wireless to the fullest.
After a simple one-tap setup, AirPods are automatically on and always connected. They sense when they're in your ears and pause when you take them out.

Simplicity from start to Siri.
Connecting to your iPhone is effortless. And you can share a song between two sets of AirPods, or even have Siri announce your messages.

Performance you'll want to hear.
Whether you're playing games or listening to music, the H1 chip delivers a stable, low-latency wireless connection for high-quality sound.
